How to Play

A secret MLB player has been chosen. Guess players by name and you will get back a similarity score (0-1000) comparing your guess to the secret player. The higher the score, the more similar the careers. 1000 means you got it.

Scoring

You start with 101 points. Each guess costs 1 point. Score floors at 0 but you can keep guessing. A correct answer scores whatever points remain, so a perfect game is 100.

Hints

Seven hints are available. Six use an escalating cost -- the first is free, then 10, 20, 30, and 40 points each, with the sixth free since you will already be at 0: Primary position, Birth year, Career WAR, Primary team, Birth country, and Initials.

The seventh hint, TOP 3, reveals the three players most similar to the secret player based on the similarity formula. It costs 25 points and is not available in Hard mode.

Difficulty

Three difficulty modes are available, selectable from the header:

  • Easy: all five badges shown per guess (position, team, birth year, WAR, country), plus Hot/Cold stat breakdown
  • Medium: only position and birth year badges shown, plus Hot/Cold
  • Hard: no badges, no Hot/Cold. Score only. Hints capped at 2, each costing 25 points. TOP 3 hint not available

Guess Badges

Badges compare your guess to the secret player. Colors:

  • Green = exact match (or very close for WAR)
  • Yellow = close but not exact
  • No color = far off

Badge logic:

  • POS: green if same primary position, yellow if guessed player played 10+ games at the secret player's position
  • TEAM: green if same franchise, yellow if same 2025 MLB division (historical teams mapped to current franchise)
  • BORN: green if exact birth year, yellow if within 5 years
  • WAR: green if within 2.0, yellow if within 10.0
  • HOME: green if same birth country only

Hot / Cold

Each guess shows which stat was most similar (Hot) and least similar (Cold) to the secret player, using stats meaningful for that position. Available in Easy and Medium modes only.

Cross-Role Guesses

If the secret player is a pitcher and you guess a position player (or vice versa), you will get a score of 0. Use that as a signal to switch sides.

Similarity Score Formula

Scores are based on the Baseball Reference similarity score methodology. For position players, career stats compared include G, AB, R, H, 2B, 3B, HR, RBI, BB, SO, SB, BA, and SLG. For pitchers: W, L, SV, IP, K, and ERA. Our scores may differ slightly from those on Baseball Reference, as their exact formula is proprietary. All stats are as of Opening Day 2026.

Player Pool

The pool includes players with:

  • Position players: 500 or more career games
  • Starting pitchers: 100 or more career starts, with the majority of appearances as a starter
  • Relief pitchers: 300 or more career appearances, with the majority in relief

Year Range

The year range filter controls which players can be chosen as the secret player. It uses each player's actual first and last year in the majors. A player is included if their career overlaps with the selected range at all. Use the preset buttons or enter custom years.

Daily vs. Unlimited

Daily mode picks the same player for everyone on a given day. Unlimited mode picks a new random player each game.

Challenge a Friend

Pick any player and generate a shareable link. Your friend will be challenged to guess that player.
